Classical Music



Ustad Amjad Ali Khan is an Indian classical sarod player, best known for his clear and fast ekhara taans. Amjad Ali Khan gave his first recital at age six. His musical heritage combines his illustrious family heritage of sarod playing with the tradition of instrumental music from Tansen and his disciples.
